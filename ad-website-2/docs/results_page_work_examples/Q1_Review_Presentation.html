🌿 Q2 Delivery Proposal

Building on Q1's foundation, our Q2 roadmap prioritizes time savings for property operations and strategic capability development initiatives that make the transition to Hub and Spoke real

February – April 2025
Priority Tier 1 Must-Have Initiatives
1

Renewals Process Transformation

Protect operational capacity through continued automation while refining requirements for Venn City integration

  • Continue resident communication automation solution
  • Workshop to prioritize backlog focused on returning time to on-site teams
  • Refine Venn City integration requirements
Why This Matters
New changes take time to embed—protecting this work gives operations teams the breathing room they need to absorb improvements and reinvest capacity into resident service
2

Hub & Spoke Capability Development

Make the centralization strategy tangible by developing one property operations function plus HR through structured 12-month development plans

  • Select one focus: Leasing & Marketing, Maintenance & Capital Projects, or Resident Services
  • HR function capability development
  • Create development plans addressing process, people, and technology
  • Support sponsors to enact plans through year-end
Timeline Context
Feb-Mar: HR development • Mid Mar-Late Apr: 1 of 3 ops functions. Hub & Spoke execution begins in 2026 with these plans as foundation
3

Community Reporting Solution Phase 2

Define and develop next phase to ensure scalability for new portfolio acquisitions and accuracy for external presentations

  • Scale reporting for nature of expanding portfolio
  • Ensure accuracy and presentation readiness
  • Prepare for potential 3rd party business development opportunities
Business Growth Driver
Positions OneWall as innovation leader while supporting faster decision-making and creating potential revenue stream through reporting-as-a-service
4

Support & Handover of Existing Solutions

Ensure successful adoption of Q1 deliverables through targeted support and knowledge transfer

  • Hiring Solution ongoing support
  • Property Transition Scripts maintenance
  • Consulting support to functional leads implementing CDF plans
Sustainability Focus
Q1 investments only deliver value if adopted—this ensures smooth transition to business-as-usual operations
Priority Tier 2 Stretch Targets
5

RAG Solution Deployment

Medium delivery effort, low change effort, high business value—chat-based knowledge base for on-site teams

  • Managers curate role-specific documentation
  • Employees access knowledge via phone (voice or text)
  • Support onboarding: "How's this done at OneWall?"
  • On-site reference: "Who are our preferred renovation vendors?"
Efficiency Multiplier
Reduces time spent searching for answers and accelerates new hire productivity through instant access to institutional knowledge
6

ADP Platform Optimization

Study current capability usage and build roadmap to unlock value while avoiding tool sprawl

  • Complete capability study (underway)
  • Build utilization roadmap
  • Redesign certain processes to fit the way ADP is built and support user adoption for true benefits realization
Change Management Consideration
New team may appreciate one more quarter to stabilize and consider their data and system requirements
7

Property Transition Process Optimization

Direct support to transition specialist to refine and optimize acquisition onboarding workflows

  • Process refinement based on recent transitions
  • Documentation updates and improvements
  • Automation opportunity identification
Portfolio Growth Enabler
Continuous improvement of transition process directly supports aggressive acquisition strategy
8

New Ideas Pipeline

Evaluate, prioritize, and schedule emerging opportunities as they arise

  • Stakeholder idea collection
  • Business value assessment
  • Resource and timeline feasibility
  • Prioritization and scheduling
Adaptive Planning
Maintains flexibility to respond to emerging needs while ensuring rigorous evaluation process

Strategic Priorities Context

Our Q2 approach balances immediate operational needs with long-term strategic capability building:

  • Established property operations and HR as primary capability development focus areas
  • Capability development defines critical processes and improvement pathways—SOP targets established, Hub & Spoke execution begins 2026
  • Priority Tier 1 represents committed work that protects Q1 investments and advances strategic transformation
  • Priority Tier 2 provides high-value stretch targets that can flex based on capacity and organizational readiness
