# Community Gallery Images

**Filenames:** `community-01.jpg`, `community-02.jpg`, `community-03.jpg`
**Component:** `src/components/CommunityGallery.jsx`
**Placement:** Card backgrounds at 40% opacity with community name, unit count, and result stat overlaid

## What to shoot / source

Exterior photography of B/C class apartment communities:

### community-01.jpg
A front-facing or angled shot of a garden-style apartment building or complex.
Shows curb appeal — landscaping, signage, parking lot, or leasing office entrance.

### community-02.jpg
A wider shot showing the scale of a community — multiple buildings, courtyard, pool area,
or aerial. Should feel operational and lived-in, not staged.

### community-03.jpg
A street-level shot showing the residential neighborhood context — sidewalks, parked cars,
trees, and surrounding homes or commercial strip. Human scale.

## Format
- Minimum 800 × 600 px (4:3 ratio preferred)
- JPG, <300 KB each
- Horizontal orientation only
